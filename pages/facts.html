Facts for First Time Travelers

Language

Japanese is the official language of Japan. However, English is generally understood in major cities with designated tourist sites.

Timezone

Japan is 9 hours ahead of GMT. All of Japan shares the same timezone and daylight savings is not practiced.

Plugs and Electricity

Japan uses plugs with two flat parallel prongs. Consider buying an adapter to ensure you can charge your electronics

Wifi

Wifi hotspots are common in major cities in Japan.

Currency

Japanese yen is accepted throughout Japan. Foreign currency can be exchanged at airports or major banks.

Tipping

Tipping is not practiced in Japan. This could cause discomfort and confusion if you do. Generally a service charge will be added onto a restuarant bill.

Credit Cards

Japan is generally a cash-centric country, but credit cards are usually accepted for shops and restuarants in major cities.

Weather

Weather in Japan can vary depending on the season. Please consider when you are traveling to be prepared.
